Happy Tots Daycare

Happy Tots offers a vibrant outdoor environment with STEM-focused activities for children aged 2-5. Located in Cityville, it emphasizes learning through play. Our certified staff includes early childhood educators with over 10 years of experience, providing a safe and stimulating environment with a low child-to-teacher ratio.

Little Learners Center

Little Learners focuses on art and small group activities for ages 1-4. Situated in Createtown, it provides a nurturing space for early development. The center boasts art specialists and bilingual staff, offering programs in creative arts, language development, and sensory play to foster holistic growth.

Sunshine Kids Daycare

Sunshine Kids features music lessons and extended hours for children 6 months to 5 years. Based in Playburg, it promotes joyful learning and exploration. With music therapists on staff and flexible scheduling options, the center supports working families while emphasizing emotional and cognitive development through play-based curricula.
